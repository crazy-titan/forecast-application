Manual Mode — Parameter Reference Guide

Not sure what values to enter? Here's a plain-English guide to every parameter below.

Horizon
How many future steps to predict.
Safe range: 7–90. Start with 14 (2 weeks of daily data).
Season Length
The repeating cycle in your data.
Daily data = 7 (weekly) · Monthly data = 12 (yearly) · Hourly = 24
CV Windows
How many times the model self-tests its accuracy before forecasting.
Keep at 5. Increase to 10 for more reliable error metrics.
p — AR Order
How many past time steps the model looks at to make its next prediction (its "memory").
Recommended: 1–3. Setting to 0 disables this component.
d — Differencing
How many times the data is "de-trended" to make it stable for modelling.
Recommended: 1 (most data has a trend). Use 0 if data is already flat/stable.
q — MA Order
How much the model learns from its own past prediction errors.
Recommended: 1–2. Setting all three (p, d, q) to 0 will cause a CV error.
P, D, Q (Seasonal)
Same as p, d, q but applied to the seasonal cycle you set above.
Defaults (P=1, D=1, Q=1) work for most seasonal datasets.
Lead Time
How many days it takes for a new order to arrive at your warehouse after you place it.
Example: if your supplier ships in 5 days, enter 5.
Service Level
How often you want to guarantee you have stock available (probability).
95% is the industry standard. 99% means very little risk but more safety stock.
Setting p, d, and q all to 0 will cause the CV engine to fail and produce no reliability score. Always keep at least one non-zero.

Model comparison (MAE)
What this shows: Mean Absolute Error (MAE) measures the strict average mathematical distance between the model's predictions and actual historical observations. A lower MAE indicates a superior model with tighter, more accurate forecasting precision.
Residual diagnostics
Ljung‑Box test will appear after forecast.
What this shows: Residuals display the pure mathematical "misses" of the model. A perfect model leaves trailing white noise centered exactly on the zero axis. Significant clustering or patterns suggest underlying seasonality that the model failed to capture.
